FAQs

Why does my Surface Pro keep blowing up?

We have looked into this issue thoroughly and it turns out that there isn't much of an answer for this and it isn't a common issue. A series of misfortunate events led to the rumour that the Surface Pro is a dangerous device. In the new 2020 edition of the Surface Pro we can assure our customers that nothing of the kind will happen and that the new model is 100% safe with built-in failsafe systems to stop it from overheating.

Is there an insurance policy for the Surface Pro if it is stolen?

Before the 2020 model of the Surface Pro, there was no complimentary insurance policy for the entire Surface range, however in the 2020 version, we decided that Surface Pro users exclusively shall recieve a complimentary insurance policy with our insurance provider, HillCorp Insurance. This is only for Surface Pro devices and other devices in HillCorp's Pro range.

What are the most useful keyboard shortcuts on a HillCorp Computer?

All HillCorp computers follow the global conventions for keyboard shortcuts. You can find those on the internet on seach engines.

Is buying a HillCorp Computer a good future investment?

HillCorp cannot control resell prices but we can say that our prices will always be reasonable and the lowest we can possibly get them to make sure that these products are easily accessible to anyone and everywhere. HillCorp products from 20 years ago have significantly increased in value since being taken off the market and have the possibility to continue to rise.

My AVAbook isn't connecting to my other AVA products.

All AVA products should be dymanically connectable. If you are having problems with this, please contact our support team here.

Devices

FAQs

Why is my touchscreen not working?

There are many possible reasons for a broken touchscreen. The heat sensor could be broken. The screen could be water damaged. There could be a crack in the screen. Any of these broken parts or problems can be fixed by using our HillCare programme.

How do I install AVA on my phone or tablet?

To make the Tokyo range accessible to everyone, we tried to keep costs as low as possible. This meant that we could not include the computer power required to run the AVA programme whilst also keeping the price down. However, the Avalanche Phones and Manhattan Tablets are completely compatible with AVA and its features. For help with setting up AVA on your phone or tablet, contact our support team here.

How do I access HillCorp Infinity features on my phone or tablet?

First, you must create or log in to your HillCorp Infinity account, then you must download the app if it is not already installed. From there, you will be able to access all the premium features.

How do I get the HillCare package with my phone or tablet?

The HillCare package is available when you buy the product. If you bought the product second-hand, you might not be signed up with HillCare. You can link HillCare easily to your Infinity account in your settings on your device.

What is the difference between the Tokyo and Avalanche range?

The Tokyo range of phones was HillCorp's attempt at making the cheapest high-quality phone on the market. Its low price comes at the cost of a few features like AVA but that does not hinder the quality of the Tokyo range. The Avalanche range is the highest-quality range of phones on the market and are compatible with AVA.

FAQs

Is my Fayonette car compatible with upgrades?

The Fayonette car is very delicate and although it has great power, messing with the system is risky. It would be recommended that you go to a mechanic for these upgrades.

Are all HillCorp cars electric?

All of our cars are electric. We do have low-line cars in our dealerships that are fuel-powered but we only advertise our electric cars to make sure we stay in check with our green image.

Which HillCorp cars are self-driving?

The HillCorp Fayonette comes with a self-driving feature and can be turned off but an extension is required for the Botticelli and Astro cars.

Where are the special offers for HillCorp Cars

Sign up to our weekly Infinity newsletter if you wish to recieve updates on special offers around our system. HillCorp Infinity account is required to use special deals and offers.

Is the self-driving feature on my Fayonette safe?

HillCorp has taken the Fayonette model under rigorous tests to make sure that it is a safe part in our community and to make sure that it eliminates all road-related deaths.
