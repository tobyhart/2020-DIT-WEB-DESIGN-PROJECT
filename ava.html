Ethics and Queries

In the modern age, the question of robots taking over and ending the human race has been posed many times. Whether it be in a big-budget sci-fi film, or in a dinner-table discussion, there is definitely a rising fear of technology becoming too powerful for us to control. However, every AVA product is encrypted and is connected to the HillCorp mainframe which can be shut off at any time if the situation gets out of control. Our engineers have made sure that AVA will never do harm to a human. Plenty of work has gone into making sure AVA is safe. Plenty of privacy issues have been brought up as well and our engineers have come up with a way that the privacy data is all stored internally and has 0% chance of being remotely accessed. The main purpose of AVA is to take humanity into the future and leave us more time for the things that matter in life.
